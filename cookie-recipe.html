Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

This recipe is from my dad and they are a favorite among friends and family.The secret ingredient is the coconut! Be warned, these will fly off of the plate!

History of cookies

Most people don't know the hilarious and bewildering past of the cookie. They were invented during a pre-destination paradox by a chef from the last 21st Century stranded in the 12th. Upon tasting them, people asked "These are delicious! How did you make them!?" and, without thinking, she casually responded "Easy". Thus they became known as 'cook-easy', but the 'y' was eventually dropped, resulting in the now-known "cook-ease".

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips
  • Oatmeal
  • Coconut

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325.
  2. Beat the butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla together until creamy.
  3. Mix together the flour, baking soda and salt in a separate bowl.
  4. Add flour mixture to butter mixture slowly.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips, oatmeal and coconut.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
